Our Firm

Lau & Choi, P.C. is a distinguished legal firm specializing in comprehensive immigration representation. Our seasoned attorneys skillfully navigate clients through every facet of the immigration process, demonstrating expertise from visa applications abroad to the intricate stages of adjustment of status within the United States. We excel in handling naturalization applications with precision and professionalism, ensuring seamless interactions with United States Citizenship and Immigration Services. Distinguished for our proficiency, we extend our legal process to represent clients in removal proceedings before the Immigration Court. Our commitment to excellence extends to appearances before the Board of Immigration Appeals and in the Federal Courts. With a dedicated focus on achieving favorable outcomes, Lau & Choi, P.C. stands as a trusted ally for individuals navigating the complex landscape of immigration law. Count on us for unwavering support and unparalleled legal representation as you embark on your journey through the intricacies of immigration processes.

Language Qualification

Lau & Choi, P.C., our team of legal assistants is a cornerstone of our commitment to comprehensive client support. Specializing in immigration matters, our adept legal assistants offer a unique advantage with proficiency in a diverse array of languages. This includes Mandarin, Cantonese, Korean, Vietnamese, and Spanish, ensuring effective communication and understanding with our diverse clientele. The ability to communicate in multiple languages reflects our dedication to providing inclusive and accessible legal services. This linguistic diversity empowers our clients, fostering a sense of comfort and assurance throughout their immigration journey. Whether you speak Mandarin, Cantonese, Korean, Vietnamese, or Spanish, our skilled legal assistants are here to guide you through the intricacies of immigration law with clarity and precision. At Lau & Choi, P.C., we pride ourselves on fostering a welcoming and supportive environment, where linguistic diversity is not only acknowledged but embraced as a valuable asset in delivering exceptional legal representation.
