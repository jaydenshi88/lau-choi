Compensation + Benefits

At Lau & Choi, we believe in building a total compensation and benefits program that supports every workers' journey and reflects their shared success in the business. While the program regularly evolves and scales to support big bets, our general approach aims to recognize workers who provide expertise, deliver innovations, and drive results by building structures and processes that are consistent and equitable across the entire organization.

Compensation

As people know, managing your income is crucial for success and that starts by being able to fully understand how much money you’re bringing in. We hope empowering workers with information that allows them to better understand their own role, so they have greater insight into how they can continue to pursue a meaningful career path. In 2024, we’re taking a global approach to Base Pay Range Visibility that will roll out over the year. Starting on January 1, all job postings will include the base pay salary range for each role. Before the end of the year, all workers will have access to their pay range for their current positions to understand their growth opportunities. In the simplest terms, total compensation includes your base pay + incentive compensation, which is made up of short-term and long-term bonuses.

Benefits

We’ve got you covered wherever you are. As part of the total rewards, there are key benefits that all workers can opt-in to depending on their personal needs. Benefits at Lau & Choi cover the following:

  • Health Care: Medical (including LGBTQIA+ support), dental, and vision plans that cover you as well as your spouse/domestic partner and children.
  • Family Care: Life insurance, parental leave, women’s reproductive health care, plus short and long-term disability.
  • Retirement: Lau & Choi matches retirement contributions so you can relax long after you retire.
  • Open Paid Time Off: Workers are trusted to take the time they need throughout the year.
  • Company-Wide Breaks: In addition to holidays, the entire company takes a 2-week end-of-year break and a 1-week mid-year work party break in the mountains to disconnect and recharge.
  • Wellness Fund: Workers also may have the option of receiving a monthly allowance for both themselves and any beneficiaries that can be spent on things like massages and gym memberships.
