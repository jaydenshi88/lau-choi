Our Services

Family-Based Immigration

Applications for permanent resident status based on family relationship, including:
  • Visa applications with U.S. Consulates abroad
  • Applications for Adjustment of Status with U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services
  • Applicable Waivers of Inadmissibility (I-601, I-212)

Employment-Based Immigration

Immigrant visa applications based on the immigrant’s skills and abilities, including:
  • National Interest Waiver
  • Outstanding Researcher
  • Alien with Extraordinary Ability
  • Multinational Executive
Labor certification for professionals and skilled workers, including:
  • High technology workers
  • Specialty cooks
  • Construction workers

Non-Immigrant Applications

Visa applications for nonimmigrant status, including:
  • Visitor visas (B-1/B-2)
  • Students (F-1)
  • Professionals from various disciplines (H-1B)
  • Seasonal Workers (H-2)
  • Trainees (H-3)
  • Fiancé visas (K visas)
  • Religious workers (R-1)
  • Professional status for Canadians and Mexicans (TN)
  • Change of Nonimmigrant Status
  • Extension of Stay

Business/Commerical

  • Nonimmigrant Business Investors (E-1/E-2)
  • Outstanding Researcher
  • Intra-company Transferees (L-1)
  • Immigrant Investors (EB-5)

Other Applications with USCIS

  • Naturalization
  • Asylum
  • Applications based on the Violence Against Women Act
  • U Visas for Victims of Criminal Activity
  • Temporary Protected Status (TPS)
  • NACARA
  • J Waiver

Removal (Deportation) Defense

Representation before the Immigration Court in Removal Proceedings, including:
  • Bond Hearings
  • Cancellation of Removal
  • Asylum
  • Adjustment of Status
  • Waivers of Inadmissibility
  • Criminal Matters
  • Religious workers (R-1)
  • Voluntary Departure
  • Appeals to the Board of Immigration Appeals

Federal Court

  • Writ of Habeas Corpus
  • Asylum
  • Petitions for Review before the Tenth Circuit Court of Appeals
  • Writ of Mandamus
